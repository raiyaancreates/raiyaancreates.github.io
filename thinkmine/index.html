why i built this

i wanted a place to write without notifications, formatting menus, or any other bullshit that pulls you out of flow.

thinkmine is just a blank canvas. you write. it saves. that's it.

features

  • minimalist interface — nothing but you and your text
  • 11 beautiful fonts — from serif to mono
  • calm color palettes — easy on the eyes
  • focus timer — optional pomodoro-style timer
  • auto-save — never lose your thoughts
  • keyboard shortcuts — for everything

keyboard shortcuts

  • Ctrl+S — save (prompts for location)
  • Ctrl+Plus/Minus — adjust text size
  • F11 — fullscreen
  • Esc — exit
  • Right-click canvas — change background
  • Click "Aa" — cycle fonts
  • Click "B" — toggle bold
  • Click "I" — toggle italic
